How It Works

Three steps to understanding

No complicated setup. No data science degree required.

1

Log your inputs

Track supplements, food, medication, and lifestyle factors with quick-tap logging. Scan barcodes, search the USDA database, or create custom entries in seconds. This is one half of the equation.

2

Check in with how you feel

Rate your mood, energy, and symptoms throughout the day—the other half. Quick 10-second check-ins, multiple times a day. The more often you log how you feel, the more data ReactLog has. Apple Health syncs 15+ biometrics automatically.

3

Discover what works

ReactLog's correlation engine connects your inputs to your mood and symptom data across days, weeks, and months—surfacing signals like which supplement improves your sleep or which food tanks your energy.
